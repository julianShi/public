Tokyo National Museum of Western Art

19 Nov 2012

I visited the Tokyo National Museum of Western Art. There are thousands of oil paintings about topics of the mixed viewpoints of western and eastern towards to world. I went to the New York City, San Francisco, Toronto, I visited so many museums in the world, but I cannot deny that this gallery is the best I have ever seen!
